About 4Health

Users can record their daily activities, including steps taken, hours of sleep, calories consumed, calories burned, weight, and BMI, using the health and fitness tracking app Health. In order to monitor their development over time, users can also maintain a journal of their everyday activities.

Five dignified, healthy computer science students—Luq, Joe, Nathan, Nitin, and Shay—designed 4Health.

4Health gives users the tools they need to track their fitness and health objectives in order to assist them in maintaining a healthy lifestyle. To better understand their progress and make health-related decisions, users can view their data in charts and graphs.
